Passionate Coder and Learner

Hi, I'm Charlie, I'm 23 from Dublin and I like to computer code, practice Brazilian Ji Jitzu and go on hikes!

This website is a place where I will try and record how I learn. Currently the two passions in my life are BJJ and computer coding. I've been told it takes roughly 10 years to obtain a "black belt" in both crafts. Since there is so much to learn in both crafts I become more aware of how I learn, process and assimilate new information.

I am passionate about computer coding and BJJ and I have become fascinated with discovering new patterns of learning that can be the most effective when applied to my two crafts. I have become experimental with my learning, adopting practices like finding a mentor, prioritizing learning through experimental drilling of concepts, and embracing failure as a learning tool to iterate over previous practices.

This website basically is a collection of the work I have done, my thoughts on the work I am doing, and information about me and how to contact me! I hope it makes for fun browsing!
